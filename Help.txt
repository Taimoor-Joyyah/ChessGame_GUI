CONTROLS
Use NUMPAD arrows for movement and
NUM 5 for selection
Use ESC for pause menu during game

GAME
The game is dual player game
{white and black}.
White on top of board with upper
and back on bottom of board wilh
lower letters.
White plays first.

PIECE SYMBOLS
K - King
Q - Queen
B - Bishop
N - Knight
R - Rook
W - Pawn

of possible moves on the board
